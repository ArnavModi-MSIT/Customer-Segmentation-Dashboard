🎯 Project Overview

This comprehensive project analyzes customer purchasing behavior using Recency, Frequency, and Monetary (RFM) analysis combined with advanced machine learning for churn prediction. Customers are segmented into actionable groups enabling targeted retention, engagement, and revenue optimization strategies.

Customer Segments

  • Champions: Best customers, highest value
  • Loyal Customers: Consistent purchasers
  • Potential Loyalists: High conversion potential
  • Need Attention: Largest engagement opportunity
  • At Risk: Likely to churn soon
  • Lost Customers: Inactive for extended period

Business Intelligence

Key Business Insights

📈

Revenue Optimization

Top 12% of customers (Champions) generate 48% of revenue. Targeted retention programs for this segment yield highest ROI.

🎯

Segmentation Strategy

25% of customers need attention representing largest reactivation opportunity. Customized engagement campaigns unlock significant growth potential.

🔮

Churn Prediction

ML models identify at-risk customers with 85%+ accuracy. Proactive intervention campaigns prevent revenue leakage before it occurs.

💰

Loyalty Programs

Potential Loyalists represent 18% of customer base. Conversion programs from this segment create sustainable revenue growth.

📊

Data-Driven Decisions

RFM scoring provides quantifiable metrics for customer valuation. Evidence-based segmentation replaces guesswork with actionable intelligence.

🚀

Performance Metrics

Monitor customer lifetime value, retention rates, and segment performance in real-time through interactive dashboards and automated reporting.

Methodology & Implementation

01

Data Integration

Raw data imported from Olist dataset into PostgreSQL with comprehensive data cleaning and validation.

02

Feature Engineering

RFM scores calculated with domain expertise. 25+ features engineered for ML models including time-series trends.

03

Model Training
